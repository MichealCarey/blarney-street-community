About The Rock Community Centre

The Rock Community Centre, formerly St. Mary's of the Rock National School, has been serving the Blarney Street community for over 45 years. Closed as a school in 1978, this historic building was officially opened as a community centre on 20th May 1988 by Aid. Toddy O'Sullivan, Lord Mayor.

Our centre is the heart of the Blarney Street community, providing a welcoming space for people of all ages to come together, learn, socialize, and celebrate. We offer a wide range of activities and services designed to strengthen community bonds and enrich the lives of our residents.

From our popular Men's Shed for retired gentlemen to MMA classes for fitness enthusiasts, from hall rentals for special occasions to our community events, The Rock Community Centre continues to be a vital hub of activity and connection in Cork City.

Meet Our Team

Tom Coleman

Chairman

Tom Coleman has been the heart and soul of The Rock Community Centre for over 45 years. His dedication to serving the Blarney Street community is unmatched, having witnessed the transformation of the former school into the vibrant community hub it is today. Tom's leadership has guided the centre through decades of change while maintaining its core mission of bringing people together. His warm personality and deep knowledge of the area's history make him a beloved figure in the community. When not overseeing centre operations, Tom can often be found sharing stories with visitors or lending a helping hand to anyone in need.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?

The centre is open Monday to Friday 9:00 AM - 10:00 PM, and weekends 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM for pre-booked events.

How do I book the hall?

You can book the hall by filling out our online booking form, calling us, or visiting the centre in person. We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance.

What facilities are available?

We have a main hall, kitchen facilities, parking, accessible entrance, and audio-visual equipment available for hire.

How much does it cost to rent the hall?

Hall rental rates vary depending on the time of day and duration. Please contact us for current pricing information.

Can I join the Men's Shed?

Yes! The Men's Shed is open to all retired men in the community. Just drop by during opening hours or contact us for more information.

Do you offer MMA classes for beginners?

Absolutely! Our MMA classes are suitable for all skill levels, including complete beginners. Our instructors will help you progress at your own pace.
